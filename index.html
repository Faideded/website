About This

For this project I was tasked with creating a blender scene for certain themes of work. We were given a brief and told how to do the project and what information needed to be displayed. For my theme I chose “Medieval”. Also, my Development diary was decent with a little over 30 pages of A4 and 5,300 words with detailed pictures and explanations telling the reader exactly what they want to see.

I was also tasked with producing some Concept art which i made 3 basic designs and one final design which i annotated accordingly. This only took me a few days as my art skills aren't the best.

Overall, i feel that this project taught me a lot of practical skills and gave me passion for games creation and design. However, i feel that i could have done a little better when it came to the texturing side of things.
