About me

I’m Oliver Betts, an aspiring UI/UX programmer with a long-time passion for creating digital experiences that feel clean, intuitive, and enjoyable to use. I’ve always been drawn to the space where design meets technology—where a smart layout or small interaction can make someone’s day a little easier.

I love digging into how people actually use products, spotting the moments where things feel confusing, and redesigning them into something smooth and satisfying. Whether I’m sketching out interface ideas, experimenting with layouts, or refining interactions, I’m always aiming to create experiences that look great and feel right.

I continue to grow in UI/UX, I’m constantly learning, exploring new tools, and pushing myself to design with both clarity and personality. My goal is to build interfaces that aren’t just functional, but memorable—interfaces that leave users thinking, “That was simple, and I enjoyed it.”
